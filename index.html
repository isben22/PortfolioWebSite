About Moi

Dare to be...eccentric

Hi! My name is Skittles, and my "owner" is a Rick Marshall. He is a developer and entrepreneur based out of Tampa Florida. And while he lives in the southern United States, it was not always this way. He was in the Army for decades, lived abroad for over 10 years in Italy, Germany and the UK and loves to see benefit come from hard work.

Designed to be... eclectic

For the past 30 years, we have been focused on delivering solutions to problems. SOLUTIONS as I have never been interested in things that did not deliver the expected value to the user. Whether that was an enterprise Project Management System, a Branded Web Site or even a Bradley Fighting System. If you are not delivering value, THEN WHY START?Solutions also come from a diversification of thought and ideas.

Developed to be... extraordinary

I believe that extraordinary outcomes stem from efficient execution of the basics. Just like the elite military teams entrusted with their countries most difficult missions, seldom train on advanced techniques until the basics are mastered. This is the acceptance of the reality that mastery of a profession is through the command of its building blocks. Rick thinks this is true as well.

Destined to be... efficacious

This brings me back to the beginning. I achieve the desired effects through mastery of the basic components such as architecture, operations and data. Not one of these aspects brings success of itself, but is the orchestration of component parts.
